Hi, Thanks for using Pocket Lists!

When inside a particular to-do list, swipe a to-do left or right to define hierarchy. Enable edit mode (tap on three-striped icon at the bottom) if you wish to move nested to-dos to other parents.

Also, when adding to-dos, you may add spaces before to-do names (just a regular space symbol before the to-do name) which will automatically define hierarchy right at the moment of adding a to-do.

For now there is no way to start adding new to-dos into the hierarchy (in the middle of the list), but we plan to integrate such ability in one of the next app updates.
